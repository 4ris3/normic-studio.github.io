Privacy Policy for Water Helper+

Effective Date: [April 21, 2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how [Dawid Stachowicz] ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Water Helper+ mobile application (the "App"). We are the data controller for the information processed under this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

1. Information You Provide Directly (Stored Locally)

When you use the App, you may provide certain information which is stored only locally on your device using standard operating system features (like UserDefaults). We do not transmit this data off your device. This includes:

  • Your daily water intake goal (e.g., 2000ml).
  • Your current water intake progress.
  • Notification start and end times for reminders.
  • Custom amounts for the "Quick Add" buttons.

This data remains on your device and is not collected by us or shared with third parties by us. It is used solely for the core functionality of the App.

2. Information Collected Automatically (Third-Party Partners - Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display advertisements. To make this possible, Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically from your device. This may include:

  • Identifiers: Such as your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA, if you provide consent via the App Tracking Transparency framework) or other device-level IDs.
  • Location: Coarse location data (approximate location) inferred from your IP address or other signals.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with ads shown in the App (e.g., ad views, clicks), and interaction with the App itself related to ad serving.
  • Diagnostics: Crash data and performance data related to the functioning of the AdMob SDK within the App.

This data is collected and processed by Google. For more details on the data Google collects and how they use it, please refer to their policies linked below.

How We Use Information and Lawful Basis for Processing

We process information for the following purposes, relying on the specified legal bases under GDPR for users in the EU/EEA:

  • To Provide App Functionality:
    • Data Used: Information you provide directly (stored locally).
    • Purpose: To enable the core features of the App (tracking water, reminders, settings). This processing occurs entirely on your device.
    • Lawful Basis: Not applicable as data is not collected/processed by us off-device.
  • To Display Third-Party Advertisements (via Google AdMob):
    • Data Used: Identifiers, Coarse Location, Usage Data, Diagnostics (as collected by Google).
    • Purpose: To display advertisements in the App, which helps support the App's development and availability. Ads may be personalized based on the collected data if consent is given, or non-personalized otherwise.
    • Lawful Basis: Consent (obtained via the ATT prompt for personalized ads/tracking, or via Google's own consent mechanisms if applicable for non-personalized ads under GDPR/ePrivacy) or Legitimate Interest (for displaying contextual/non-personalized ads where consent is not the required basis, and for essential operational aspects like frequency capping or fraud prevention).
  • For Analytics and Measurement (by Google AdMob):
    • Data Used: Identifiers, Coarse Location, Usage Data, Diagnostics.
    • Purpose: For Google to analyze ad performance, measure audience engagement, and improve their advertising services.
    • Lawful Basis: Consent (where applicable for measurement related to personalized ads) or Legitimate Interest (for aggregated analytics and measurement not requiring individual consent).
  • To Maintain and Improve Services (by Google AdMob):
    • Data Used: Diagnostics (Crash Data, Performance Data).
    • Purpose: For Google to maintain and improve the stability and performance of the AdMob SDK.
    • Lawful Basis: Legitimate Interest (ensuring the technical functionality and stability of the advertising service).

Advertising and Tracking Choices

The App uses Google AdMob to serve ads. If you have provided consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework (for iOS users) or other applicable consent mechanisms, Google may use your device's Advertising Identifier (e.g., IDFA) to serve personalized ads and for tracking purposes across different apps and websites.

You can manage your preferences regarding personalized advertising and tracking:

  • Through the ATT prompt when first launching the App (iOS users).
  • Within your device's system settings (e.g., Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking or Advertising on iOS; similar settings on Android).
  • Through Google's ad settings or other opt-out mechanisms they provide.

Your Data Protection Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data that is processed.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request correction of any information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to be Forgotten'): You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request the restriction of the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request the transfer of the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

Please note that for data collected automatically by Google AdMob, you should primarily exercise these rights directly with Google, as they are the controller for that data processing in many respects. You can find information on how to manage your data with Google through their privacy policies linked above. For requests related specifically to data we might control (which in this case is minimal beyond facilitating AdMob integration), you can contact us using the details below.
